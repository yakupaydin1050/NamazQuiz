### Tam Açıklama — İngilizce (maks. 4000 karakter)

How well do you know Islamic prayer? Namaz Quiz is a fun and educational quiz app designed for those who want to deepen their knowledge of Salah (Islamic prayer) — one of the five pillars of Islam.

📚 595+ QUESTIONS
Challenge yourself with a comprehensive question bank covering the obligatory acts, conditions, pillars, prayer times, purification rules, and much more about Salah.

🎯 3 DIFFICULTY LEVELS
• Easy — Basic concepts and everyday knowledge
• Medium — More detailed jurisprudence
• Hard — Advanced topics and subtle distinctions

⚡ SPEED BONUS
Answer quickly to earn bonus points! The faster you think, the more you score.

🏆 RANK SYSTEM
Earn points and climb the ranks:
💤 Sleeping Row → 🧎 Front Row Candidate → 📿 Prayer Bead Holder → 🕌 Mosque Star → 🎙️ Call to Prayer Master → 📖 Fatwa Machine → 👑 Prayer Legend

🌍 LEADERBOARD
Compete with players worldwide, claim your spot in the rankings, and write your name at the top.

📖 BUILT-IN GLOSSARY
Tap any highlighted term in a question to see a clear, concise explanation. Fard, Ruku, Sujud, Tayammum, and dozens more Islamic terms explained instantly.

🌙 DAILY HADITH
A new Hadith every day — sourced, meaningful, and inspiring.

✏️ SKIP / REVEAL ANSWER
Don't get stuck on a question you don't know. Skip it or reveal the answer in learning mode — without affecting your score.

🔒 FREE & SAFE
No ads. No hidden fees. Completely free.

---

Namaz Quiz is ideal for students, new Muslims, and anyone who wants to refresh or deepen their knowledge of Islamic prayer. Learn while you play — and play your way to the top!
